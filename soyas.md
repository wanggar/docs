## 1) The problem

Most language products optimize for **studying** (tapping, grammar units, memorization). But the real pain is: people can “understand” yet **freeze when they need to communicate and use it**

**Core gap**

- Users don’t get enough real speaking reps with relevant feedback
- Lessons aren’t tied to what the user truly needs *right now*
- Progress feels fake because it’s not measured in real-life conversation ability

---

## 2) Vision

Learn a language by living it.

## 3) Product definition

Soyas helps learners reach conversational fluency by getting them to talk, then teaching from that exact conversation. 
A bilingual speaking partner who understands how you think and express yourself — helping you become your true self in another language.

懂你母语、兴趣和表达方式的双语口语伙伴，帮你成为另一种语言里的自己。

## 5) Target user + ICP

### Broad target category (who this is generally for)

<aside>

Non‑native speakers who can understand targeted languages, but are not yet conversationally fluent in real-time speaking.

</aside>

**Common traits:**

- They can read and follow most conversations.
- They know "the rules," but speech breaks down under speed, pressure, or uncertainty.
- They want to improve by **talking more**, not by doing textbook units.

### Primary focus segment (ICP: who we build for first)

<aside>

Non‑native speakers living and working in their non‑native language environment who need the language for both productivity and social connection.

</aside>

### First wedge (initial beachhead)

<aside>

International people working in the tech industry (software, product, data, design)  & living in an English-speaking environment.

</aside>

### Who we are **not** for (initially)

- Test-prep-first learners (IELTS/TOEFL as primary goal)
- People who mainly want reading/writing practice
- Absolute beginners (A0–A2) who need heavy translation and structured basics
