Soyas helps learners reach **conversational fluency** by getting them to talk, then teaching from that exact conversation: a bilingual speaking partner who understands how you think and express yourself—helping you become your true self in another language.

## The problem

Most language products optimize for **studying** (tapping, grammar units, memorization). The real pain is understanding the language yet **freezing when you need to communicate**. Soyas focuses on real speaking reps, feedback tied to what you need *right now*, and progress you feel in real conversation—not only in lessons.

## Product promise

- “I can speak more comfortably in real situations.”
- “I’m improving every day without studying.”
- “The app gets me—my topics, my mistakes, my vibe.”

## Who it’s for

Soyas is built for **non-native speakers** who can follow the language but are not yet comfortable speaking in real time—especially people **living and working** in an environment where they need the language every day. An initial focus is **international tech professionals** in English-speaking environments.

Soyas is **not** aimed first at test-prep-only learners, people who mainly want reading/writing practice, or absolute beginners who need heavy translation and structured basics.

## Key experiences

- **Instant talk** — Start speaking right away in a low-friction environment.
- **Grounded feedback** — Feedback tied to what you actually said.
- **Progress you can feel** — Improvement measured in how you show up in conversation.
