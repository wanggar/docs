WordShip helps you memorize new words through natural conversations with AI. Instead of using traditional flashcards, you learn words by using them in real discussions about topics you care about.

## How It Works

1. **Smart Word Selection**
   - The app selects 7 new words per session based on your level
   - You can customize your word list before starting
   - Words are tracked and presented based on your learning progress

2. **Natural Learning Through Conversation**
   - Chat with an AI that naturally incorporates your target words
   - Get real-time hints if you're stuck
   - Learn how words are used in different contexts
   - Practice using new words in your responses

3. **Progress Tracking**
   - Track which words you've mastered
   - See your daily learning streaks
   - Monitor your vocabulary growth over time
   - Review words you've learned in previous sessions

## Getting Started

1. Create a free account
2. Select your language level and interests
3. Choose your learning words
4. Start chatting and learning
